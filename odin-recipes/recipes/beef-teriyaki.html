Beef Teriyaki

Description

Very tasty beef teriyaki recipe. So easy to make that even I can do it.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups uncooked glutinous white rice
  • 1⅔ cups water
  • 1 pound boneless beef sirloin steak (3/4 inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon packed brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Make rice with rice cooker
  2. Meanwhile, slice beef into very thin strips.
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the cornstarch, broth, soy sauce, sugar and garlic powder. Mix thoroughly.
  4. In a large skillet, over medium/high heat, saute the beef strips until browned and juices evaporate.
  5. Stir cornstarch mixture into meat. Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring frequently. Serve over the cooked rice.
